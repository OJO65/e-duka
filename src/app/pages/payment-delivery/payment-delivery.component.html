Delivery

Kisumu — Local Delivery

Orders within Kisumu town and surrounding areas are delivered directly by our team. Delivery fees, where applicable, are confirmed before checkout.

Nationwide — Courier

For customers outside Kisumu, we arrange courier delivery on a case-by-case basis depending on your location and the item. We'll discuss courier options and costs with you directly after your order is placed.

In-Store Pickup

Prefer to collect yourself? You're welcome to pick up your order directly from our shop in Kisumu at no extra cost.
